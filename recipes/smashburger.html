Smashburger Recipe

Description

This recipe instructs you on how to make my favorite type of burger, the smashburger. A quick and esy way to make a comforting sandwich with crispy edges and dripping in grease.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 lbs 80/20 ground beef
  • potato roll
  • mayo
  • salt
  • pepper
  • a good melting cheese, such as american
  • high smoke point oil, such as canola

Steps

  1. preheat a cast iron skillet over medium high heat
  2. add approximately 1 tbs of canola oil to skillet
  3. roll beef into a ball, place beef into pan and press flat with a solid turner
  4. salt patty generously
  5. toast bun and prepare with mayo
  6. flip patty after 2 minutes
  7. salt and pepper patty and place slice of cheese
  8. remove patty from skillet and place in bun, cover with bun top
