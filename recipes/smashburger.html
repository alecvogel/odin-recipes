Smashburger Recipe

Description

This recipe instructs you on how to make my favorite type of burger, the smashburger. A quick and esy way to make a comforting sandwich with crispy edges and dripping in grease.

Ingredients

    1/4 lbs 80/20 ground beef potato roll mayo salt pepper a good melting cheese, such as american high smoke point oil, such as canola

Steps

    preheat a cast iron skillet over medium high heat add approximately 1 tbs of canola oil to skillet roll beef into a ball, place beef into pan and press flat with a solid turner salt patty generously toast bun and prepare with mayo flip patty after 2 minutes salt and pepper patty and place slice of cheese remove patty from skillet and place in bun, cover with bun top
