Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

This recipe instructs you on how to make my fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken thigh/li>
  • baking powder
  • cornstartch
  • salt
  • pepper
  • seasoning blend
  • high smoke point oil, such as canola

Steps

  1. heat oil in dutch over or fryer to 275 F
  2. mix corn starch, baking powder, salt, pepper and seasoning in bowl
  3. coat chicken in seasoned breading and allow to rest
  4. drop breaded chicken into preheated oil
  5. remove chicken from oil onto a wire rack
  6. season with salt
